Seasonality
Seasonality is a data analysis website that predicts the buying window of eBay categories in the next coming 12 months based on transaction data in last 5 years. It helps seller to sell what's popular.
Demand Analysis
eBay seller manager uses our tool "Seasonality" to predict what will be popular in next few months, then guide sellers to increase the inventory of those to-be popular goods. In a few months, those goods become popular and quickly sold out, benefiting both eBay sellers and buyers, as well as eBay platform.
